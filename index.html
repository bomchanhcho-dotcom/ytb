About African Stories

We craft short, cinematic narratives centered on everyday people confronting unjust systems—airports, HOAs, corporate boardrooms—and finding ingenious paths to dignity and change. Each episode is researched, scripted, and produced with a focus on suspense, moral clarity, and catharsis.

  • 🎯 Focus: justice‑driven arcs, high‑stakes choices, twist endings
  • 🎙️ Format: 8–18 minutes · weekly releases
  • 🌍 Perspective: Africa and the global Black diaspora
